The Phantom Thieves

Joker — The Leader

Joker is the protagonist, known for his calm demeanor and strong sense of justice. He is silent but very dedicated to his friends. In his free time he likes to play baseball,go to the sauna, and explore the Metaverse with his friends

Ann Takamaki —

Ann is a strong-willed member of the Phantom Thieves with a kind heart. She loves to go shopping, practice her modeling skills, and go to restaurants to have sweet items like pastries.

Goro Akechi – The Detective Prince

Akechi is a genius detective who hides a darker side beneath his charming smile. Jazz is a favorite music genre of his, he's always doing interviews. He investigates the Phantom Thieves for about 8 months and begins a rivalry with Joker along the way
