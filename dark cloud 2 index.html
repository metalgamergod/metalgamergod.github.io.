Dark Cloud 2

Plot Summary

Dark Cloud 2 (also known as Dark Chronicle) is an action role-playing game that follows Max, a boy with a knack for invention, and Monica, a princess from the future. Together, they must stop Emperor Griffon from destroying the timeline. Using a time-traveling train and a magical camera, they travel through dungeons, rebuild the world with "Georama," and fight to preserve both the past and the future.

Main Characters

  • Max: The young inventor from Palm Brinks. He fights with a wrench and a hammer. With his atlamillia stone he can travel to the future.
  • Monica:A sword-wielding princess from 100 years in the future. She also uses magic arm bands to cast spells, her Atlamillia stone allows her to travel to the past.
  • Throughout the game both Max and Monica travel through time battling monsters and making their way to Emperor Griffon who is trying to rewrite history in his own image.
